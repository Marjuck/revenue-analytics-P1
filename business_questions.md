# Business Questions and Insight Angles

## 1. Total Revenue
- What is the total revenue in the dataset?
- What is the monthly revenue trend?
- Are there any obvious seasonality patterns?

## 2. Revenue by Geography
- Which countries contribute the most revenue?
- Is revenue highly concentrated in a single market?

## 3. Revenue by Customer
- Who are the top customers?
- What percentage of total revenue is generated by the top 10 customers?
- Is the business exposed to concentration risk?

## 4. Repeat Purchase Behavior
- How many customers placed more than one invoice?
- What is the repeat purchase rate?

## 5. Monetization Quality
- What is average revenue per customer?
- Which customers are high-value vs low-value?
- How should retention efforts be prioritized?

## 6. RFM-style Base Table
- Can we build recency, frequency, and monetary metrics for downstream segmentation?
- Which customers appear valuable enough for targeted retention or upsell?
